# Reflection Notes

## What This Pack Does Well
- Balances AI assistance with human control.
- Tells a clear ROI story for real estate and any service business.
- Treats duplicate suppression as a first-class requirement.

## Trade-Offs
- Drafting only, not auto-send, reduces demo flash but avoids compliance and brand-risk issues.
- Airtable adds one more system, but it gives a cleaner audit trail than relying only on CRM history.

## What Would Change for Production
- Add SLA-driven owner assignment and follow-up timers.
- Add enrichment from approved third-party data providers.
- Add brand-specific scoring prompts and A/B prompt versioning.

## Most Impactful Future Improvement
- Introduce an approval timer with escalation so unworked qualified leads trigger reminders automatically.

## Wow Factor
- Add an auto follow-up sequence that reminds the assigned rep if a high-score lead has no human action within the SLA window.
