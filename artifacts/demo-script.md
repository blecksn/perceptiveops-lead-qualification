# Demo Script

## Opening
Show how a service business can turn raw inbound interest into a visible, prioritized lead pipeline without auto-sending risky outreach.

## Happy Path
1. Submit a fake lead form with complete details and clear buying intent.
2. Show the workflow validating fields and checking for duplicates.
3. Show the AI score, rationale, and follow-up draft.
4. Show the new/updated HubSpot contact and Airtable audit record.
5. Explain the measurable value: faster response and less manual triage.

## Failure / Edge Case Path
1. Submit the same lead again.
2. Show the dedup guard preventing a second CRM record.
3. Submit a lead with vague content and missing phone number.
4. Show the record routed to `manual_review` instead of forcing a bad score.

## Key Talking Points
- AI is used only after cheap validation and dedup.
- Human approval stays in the loop for outbound messaging.
- Every failure state is visible in Airtable.

## Close
Highlight portability: replace the intake form and CRM, keep the same decision pattern.
